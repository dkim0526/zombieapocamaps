Communicate with Others

Click to go to Chat Room

Have a means of communication to make sure everyone is safe and accounted for. Reach out to other survivors to form a team and fight against the apocalypse together. The more people you are with, the better your chances of survival are. Don't fight the apocalypse alone!

Develop Survival Skills

Learning skills such as hunting or building could save your life if the situation becomes perilous enough. Having these skills will make you more independent and thus more likely to survive.

Have an Escape Plan

Click to go to Locations

During an apocalypse, anything can happen that will force you to evacuate. Figuring out beforehand where you will take shelter, where you will get food, and which route you will take can keep you from falling prey to the dangers around you.

Find a Safe Zone

Shelter is one of the most critical aspects of your survival plan. Find a place that is as safe as possible while still being close to the resources you will need to survive. Having a spot mapped out will allow you to also make preparations before it is too late.
