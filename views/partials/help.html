Understanding the Survival Guide

The Survival Guide will help you figure out how to better prepare for a zombie apocalypse. It provides helpful hints and tips to survive the zombie apocalypse and possibly natural disasters like earthquakes, floods, and hurricanes.
There are two ways to explore the room to find the survival tips:

  • Click on the names of the survival tips on the sidebar on the right to reveal more information about that tip.
  • Click on the colored circles on the items lying around in the room to reveal more information about the survival tip associated with that item.
Searching for safe zones in San Diego

This tab will help you find the safest areas to go in the case of a zombie apocalypse. How do we determine this? Answer We use a formula based off of these factors:

  • Population Density: The higher the population, the more chances that there will be more infected. We think that avoiding these high population areas will give you a better chance to not get infected.
  • Crime Rate: More crime = more danger. Dangerous areas will most likely have more looters and violent people in the area. Getting away from these crime intensive areas will make you safer.
  • Death Rate: The more people that are dying, the more likely there will be more infected zombies. These death rates from Delphi include crime and sickness related deaths. Staying away from sickness and crime will keep you safe

Formula: crime + death + population = rating
The higher the rating, the more dangerous an area

Finding nearby resources

To find directions between two locations:

  • Type in the origin location and destination in the white textbox in the upper lefthand corner.
  • Change your way of transportation with the neighboring blue box labelled "Walking", "Transit", and "Driving".
To find directions between your current location to a location of the specific resource:
  • Choose between the three resources (Food and Water, Health, Supplies) and click on the type of resource.
  • After the markers of the specific resource are loaded onto the map, click on the location you want to go to.
  • Directions will show up on the lower righthand corner.
What is the checklist?:
  • The checklist is a list of recommended items that you might need for a zombie apocalypse. To help you find these items, the items are color coded by the type of resource it is. If you found an item at a location, simply click on the checkbox and Update to save your checklist
