About Zombie ApocaMaps

It's hard to prepare for an event that is unexpected, and existing preparedness sites are not designed to keep users interested. With an easy way to connect with others, explore areas in San Diego, scavenge for limited and nearby resources, and learn necessary survival tips, Zombie ApocaMaps will be your top choice in helping you prepare for natural disasters in a fun way!

How prepared are you?

Search for Safety Zones

Locate areas in San Diego where it would be safe to be! Search by district and see what district in San Diego has the best rating. These ratings are calculated based off of our advanced algorithm using real world data.

Find Nearby Resources

Find resources (food, water, health, and supplies) near you. We use GPS tracking to get your up to date location. Either by clicking the location marker or through search, you can find directions from your location to the resource. When you have the resources, you can check them off and update your checklist so you know what you have at all times.

Connect With Other Users

Connect with everyone using Zombie Apocamaps. We have created a forum for you to post questions and find new people. These forums are separated by sub category so you can find out what you want.

Learn handy tips from the Survival Guide

A survival guide that will teach you how to survive a zombie apocalypse. It will include helpful tips on how to create an evacuation plan, find nearby supplies, fight zombies, and much more!
